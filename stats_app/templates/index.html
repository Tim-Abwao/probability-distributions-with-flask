Probability Distribution Sampler

This simple web app helps explore some useful probability distributions; and showcases the functionality of Flask, SciPy, Pandas, Matplotlib and Seaborn.

The idea is to enable anyone to:

  • get basic facts about a probability distribution
  • specificy parameters of choice
  • generate a downloadable sample, and
  • view graphs and summary statistics.
